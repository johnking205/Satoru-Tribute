Satoru Iwata

“On my business card, I am a corporate president. In my mind, I am a game developer. But in my heart, I am a gamer.”

— Satoru Iwata

History

  • 1959 - Born the son of a prefectural official.
  • 1975 - Started developing his first games on his HP 65 that he purchased with his own money.
  • 1978 - Admitted to Tokyo Institute of Technology
  • 1980 - While attending university, joined HAL Laboratory as a programmer
  • 1982 - Hired by HAL Laboratory after graduating, his father did not talk to him for 6 months after this event
  • 1983 - Worked on his first game developed for the NES, Joust
  • 1993 - Promoted to president of HAL
  • 1999 - Developed a compression tool that allowed the original Kanto region to be included in Pokemon Gold and Silver
  • 2002 - Named president of Nintendo
  • 2003-2009 - Led the revitalization of the company with the NDS and Wii
  • 2010-2014 - Oversaw a downturn in Nintendo's fortunes do the commercial failure of the WiiU
  • 2014-2015 - Oversaw the development of the now wildly successful Nintendo Switch that would turn Nintendo's fortunes around.
  • 2015 - Passed away due to complications from a tumor
